Colin Bradley is a serial manipulator and con artist who spent five years systematically destroying the life and finances of his former partner, Lorelle. After meeting her online in 2014, Bradley weaponized her trust to fund his fictitious lifestyle as a supposed V8 race car driver. He drained her savings, coerced her into taking a crippling $60,000 loan against her home, and committed identity fraud by opening a failing business, Big Rig 4x4, entirely in her name. While secretly pursuing multiple other women, Bradley racked up over $100,000 in illicit loans under Lorelle's identity, ultimately forcing her into bankruptcy, displacing her children, and costing her the family home.

This case matters because it exposes a highly dangerous, calculated pattern of severe financial and emotional abuse. Bradley left Lorelle destitute and publicly defamed, stole her personal belongings, and even made her purchase her own $8,000 engagement ring. He immediately moved on to exploit other women using identical lies about nonexistent trust funds and future wealth. Documenting his predatory playbook is essential to holding him legally accountable for fraud and preventing him from destroying the lives of future victims.
