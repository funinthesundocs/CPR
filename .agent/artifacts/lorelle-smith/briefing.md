## Executive Summary

This briefing document outlines the systematic financial, emotional, and psychological abuse inflicted upon **Lorelle** by **Colin James Bradley** over a multi-year period beginning in **2014**. Operating under the guise of a romantic partner and business entrepreneur, **Colin** engaged in identity fraud, financial manipulation, and severe deceit that ultimately drove the victim into bankruptcy. By exploiting **Lorelle** for housing, personal loans, and credit, he accumulated over **$100,000** in unpaid debts, caused the loss of her home, and publicly ruined her reputation. This case file synthesizes the core elements of his fraudulent activities, demonstrating a calculated strategy of securing funds through false promises and leaving victims with absolute financial devastation.

## Defendant Profile

**Colin James Bradley** presents himself as an ambitious entrepreneur and former V8 super Ute race driver, using these personas to manipulate victims into funding his lifestyle and business ventures. He operates by convincing others to invest in his supposed potential, often utilizing a fabricated story about possessing a multi-million dollar trust fund to guarantee repayment. During his time with **Lorelle**, he transitioned from a building labourer to attempting to run various automotive and construction businesses, such as Big Rig Tyres and Big Rig 4x4. His profile is that of a serial manipulator who habitually engages in infidelity, secretly dating other women like **Sally** and **Kelly**, and frequently brings them into shared homes under absurd pretenses, such as claiming his romantic partner is merely his step-sister.

## How the Relationship Began

The relationship commenced online in **2014** while **Lorelle** was residing in New South Wales. **Colin** quickly leveraged her professional position by asking her to organize the purchase of a Holden Colorado and a Captiva through her dealership. During their early phone conversations, he manipulated her empathetic nature by claiming his ex-partner, **Jennifer**, owed him over **$200,000**. Taking advantage of her kindness, he repeatedly asked to borrow money for basic living expenses like rent and bills. By the middle of **2014**, **Lorelle** moved in with him in Queensland, securing employment to financially support them both while he abandoned his work as a building labourer. In **November 2014**, he proposed marriage, though he manipulated **Lorelle** into paying **$8,000** for her own engagement ring.

## Promises and Deception

Throughout the relationship, **Colin** maintained control through a continuous stream of false promises and elaborate lies. He assured **Lorelle** that his business ventures would secure their financial future and repeatedly promised to repay borrowed funds using his nonexistent multi-million dollar trust fund. When he convinced her to take out a **$60,000** loan against her house, he explicitly promised to make monthly repayments of **$3,500**, a commitment he completely ignored. His deception extended deeply into their personal life; he would claim to be away in Melbourne during the weekdays working for a Milton-based building company, when in reality he was spending time with another woman named **Sally**. He continuously hid his affairs, forged business operations in her name without her consent, and routinely fabricated stories to cover his financial and romantic indiscretions.

## Financial Impact

The financial devastation inflicted upon **Lorelle** is profound and far-reaching. The exploitation began with around **$10,000** in direct personal loans for his daily expenses. It rapidly escalated when **Colin** orchestrated a **$60,000** loan against her New South Wales home, which went entirely unpaid, resulting in the forced sale of the property and displacing her adult children. He secretly took out multiple online loans in her name, pushing her into bankruptcy when creditors pursued her for over **$100,000** in unpaid debts. He drained her income by using her wages and commissions to cover his failing business expenses. Furthermore, when he finally abandoned the relationship, he left her with **$0**, stealing her personal belongings including a jet ski, camper trailer, Triton ute, television, and refrigerator. In total, alongside the **$8,000** engagement ring she purchased herself, the financial ruin he orchestrated was absolute.

## Timeline of Events

The exploitation began in **2014** when the two met online, leading to early financial borrowing and cohabitation in Queensland. In **2015**, **Colin** launched Big Rig Tyres and convinced **Lorelle** to take out the catastrophic **$60,000** house loan to fund his employees and his racing ambitions at the Gold Coast 500. By **2016**, Big Rig Tyres had gone bankrupt, prompting him to fraudulently open Big Rig 4x4 solely in her name, moving the shop from Rocklea to Ipswich and then to Slacks Creek due to unpaid rent. Throughout **2017**, he secretly acquired numerous online loans in her name, leading to her being served bankruptcy papers after they returned from a cruise in **2018**. It was also in **2018** that he faked a job in Melbourne to spend time with **Sally**, ultimately moving out and stealing **Lorelle**'s personal assets. Despite his departure, he continued to harass her throughout **2019** with abusive texts and false claims of having investors to repay her, culminating in messages in **December 2020** where he promised her a new car and the **$80,000** he explicitly acknowledged owing her.

## Evidence and Documentation

A robust collection of documentation supports the claims of fraud and financial abuse. The case file includes official loan agreements for the **$60,000** house loan, extensive bankruptcy filing papers, and formal business registration documents for both Big Rig Tyres and Big Rig 4x4 that prove the unauthorized use of the victim's identity. Financial records feature bank statements demonstrating the initial **$10,000** transfers to the defendant. Furthermore, there is substantial digital evidence, including screenshots of severe public defamation from the Logan Crime Watch group and the malicious "Been Rigged by Big Rig 4x4" page. Communication logs, including text messages and Facebook interactions, provide written proof of his unfulfilled repayment promises, his aggressive threats, and his deceptive sponsorship attempts for car racing.

## Witness Testimony

The case is bolstered by the potential testimonies of numerous individuals who witnessed or suffered from the defendant's fraudulent behavior. **Lorelle**'s adult children can testify to being displaced when their family home was sold due to his defaulted loans. Former employees of Big Rig Tyres and Big Rig 4x4 serve as crucial witnesses, as they can confirm they were hired by **Colin Bradley** but were denied proper wages, tax contributions, and superannuation. Additionally, disgruntled clients who were defrauded by the business, including those whose vehicles were stolen from the uninsured shop, can attest to the hostile environment he created, which resulted in them threatening the victim at her workplace. There is also the potential to gather corroborating testimony from his other romantic targets, such as **Sally** and his later wife **Kelly**, to demonstrate his ongoing pattern of deception.

## Pattern of Behavior

The actions of **Colin James Bradley** reveal a highly calculated, predatory pattern of behavior targeting vulnerable women for financial and housing stability. He systematically uses the promise of a shared future to gain access to credit, taking out loans under his partners' names to shield himself from liability. He operates businesses recklessly, hiring staff without paying legal entitlements like taxes or superannuation, and abandons these ventures when they inevitably fail. His romantic life is equally duplicitous, characterized by serial infidelity and the audacious gaslighting of bringing other women into his shared home. When his schemes collapse or victims demand accountability, his behavior pivots from grandiose promises of non-existent trust funds to aggressive manipulation and threats of police involvement, ensuring his victims remain intimidated and financially paralyzed.

## Why This Case Matters

This case is a critical example of how deeply financial abuse and identity fraud can destroy an individual's life. **Lorelle** was not merely financially impoverished; she was robbed of her home, her credit rating, and her personal property. Furthermore, because the defendant fraudulently placed his failing, uninsured business in her name, she bore the total brunt of public outrage, facing severe social media defamation and physical threats at her workplace from his wronged clients. Holding **Colin James Bradley** accountable is essential not only to seek justice for the total devastation of the victim's life but to prevent a documented serial con artist from continuing to prey upon new victims, employees, and consumers under the guise of legitimate business operations and romantic partnerships.
