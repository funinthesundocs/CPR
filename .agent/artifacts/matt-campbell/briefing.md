## Executive Summary

This briefing documents the extensive, multi-national fraud perpetrated by **Colin James Bradley** (also known as **Cole**), who operated under the corporate aliases **Marine Warrior Superyachts** and **Finish Line Boats**. Posing as a visionary in marine technology, **Bradley** promised revolutionary "zero-fuel" yachts and global licensing deals, claiming to have millions in funding and massive presale orders. In reality, his entire operation was an elaborate deception built on forged contracts, fictitious investors, unpaid professionals, and abandoned facilities across at least four countries: **Australia**, **Thailand**, the **UAE**, and **Vietnam**. This document, serving as the flagship case for the decentralized justice platform known as the Court of Public Record, compiles evidence, timelines, and testimonies to establish a permanent public archive of his actions to prevent future exploitation.

## How the Relationship Began

The documented relationship between **Bradley** and former consultant **W. Matthew Campbell** began entirely by chance on **September 6, 2025**, at a beach club in **Da Nang, Vietnam**. **Campbell**, who had a background in renewable energy and systems design, noticed **Bradley** wearing a hat claiming his boat gets "infinite miles to the gallon" through zero-fuel propulsion. What started as a casual conversation about alternative energy evolved into **Bradley** pitching himself as the founder of **Marine Warrior Superyachts**.

On **September 20, 2025**, **Bradley** recruited **Campbell** to be the Director of International Marketing. **Bradley** offered a salary of **$40,000/month**, equity in the company, and the authority to build out the business's global systems, presenting seemingly legitimate investor decks and claims of a **Zurich** credit line.

## The Promise and Deception

**Bradley** baited investors, professionals, and vendors with promises of a marine revolution, specifically claiming to have secured **340** yacht presales and a massive credit line from a **Zurich** bank, purportedly totaling **12.5 Million USD**. He claimed to have active manufacturing operations or partnerships in **Turkey**, **Thailand**, the **UAE**, and **Vietnam**, and referenced a partnership with an individual named **Ali** in **Turkey**.

Behind this polished facade, there was pure dysfunction and deceit. The funding lines were entirely fabricated; **Bradley** routinely blamed bank errors, compliance checks, or uncooperative attorneys for the delays in funds arriving. He forged investor agreements, falsified wire receipts, and lied about possessing legal rights to yacht designs initially sourced from **Dave** of **Mancraft Yachts**. When pressed for proof of his claims, **Bradley** could produce zero corroborating evidence.

The financial devastation left in **Bradley's** wake is vast and spans across continents. He failed to pay his teams, leaving unpaid wages, abandoned rents, and defrauded builders.

- **W. Matthew Campbell** worked without ever receiving his promised **$40,000/month** salary. To keep the chaotic operation afloat, **Campbell** paid out-of-pocket for an accountant, meals, transportation, rent, and a moped for **Bradley**.

- He faces another default judgment in the **UAE** for **680,000 dirham** for breach of contract, which could result in jail time. He also failed to pay a judicial appeal fee of **AED 16,135.50** related to a real estate case.

## Why This Case Matters

The case of **Colin James Bradley** represents a critical failure of traditional, siloed legal systems to keep pace with highly mobile, cross-border con artists who hide behind distance and jurisdiction. Because victims often cannot afford international legal battles, scammers like **Bradley** act with impunity.

This case is the genesis of the **Court of Public Record** — a decentralized, immutable justice platform built by **Bradley's** own victims. By crowdsourcing verifiable evidence and publishing it publicly, this platform ensures that **Bradley** and others like him are placed in a "digital prison." The goal is to establish a permanent reputational firewall that warns future partners, applies continuous pressure for restitution, and proves that collective transparency can succeed where traditional courts fall silent.
