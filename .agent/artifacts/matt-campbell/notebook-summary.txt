Instead, Bradley extracted six weeks of unpaid labor and thousands in out-of-pocket expenses while delivering nothing but lies about phantom funding, fake manufacturing partners, and nonexistent investors across Turkey, Thailand, UAE, and Vietnam.

When confronted with requests for proof on October 20, 2025, Bradley produced nothing — no contracts, no investors, no evidence of any of his claims. Campbell and CFO John Cooper documented the entire fraud. Bradley's trail spans four countries with verified victims in Australia, Thailand, UAE, and Vietnam, making him one of the most prolific serial fraudsters operating across Southeast Asia and the Pacific.
