## Detailed Narrative Analysis

### Phase 1: Grooming, Marriage, and Corporate Shielding (2019–2021)

The relationship began on WeChat in 2019. Bradley immediately established a narrative of victimhood, claiming his ex-wife had stolen from his company, resulting in the loss of his builder's license and liquidating his business. In reality, Bradley was an undisclosed bankrupt seeking a financial host.

Discovering that Cai owned a nearly paid-off home in Brisbane and had $300,000 in an offset account, Bradley rushed her into marriage, which took place on January 18, 2020, on South Stradbroke Island. He subsequently convinced her to open two companies under her own name—Austyle Building Pty Ltd and an investment firm—ensuring she bore all legal and financial liability while he secretly controlled the operations behind the scenes.

Bradley's arrogant and incompetent management led to the collapse of Austyle Building in November 2021, draining Cai's initial $300,000 investment. Desperate to save the business under Bradley's pressure, Cai refinanced her home in September 2021 for an additional $200,000, which was also lost.

### Phase 2: The "Family Trust" Illusion and International Evasion (2024–2025)

Following the collapse of their Australian ventures, Bradley initiated a multi-country evasion tour, entirely funded by Cai and justified by his central lie: a 150 million Euro "family trust" located in Europe. He promised Cai that if she just funded his immediate business setups, the trust would unlock, and he would repay her "15 fold".

**Thailand (March 2024):** Bradley moved to Thailand to supposedly set up a new yachting business. When he ran out of money, he claimed he was being blackmailed by Thai staff and had to flee.

**Dubai, UAE (November 2024):** Bradley relocated to Dubai to establish two new companies, claiming it was for tax purposes. It was during this period that Bradley relentlessly pressured Cai, who was now broke, to beg her sister and friends for money. He claimed Dubai banking structures required sudden, unexpected fees to release his European funds. Under intense coercion, Cai wired over $65,000 in borrowed funds.

**Vietnam (July 2025):** Failing to repay the borrowed money by his promised June 2025 deadline, and allegedly owing $680,000 AED in Dubai, Bradley fled to Vietnam, again promising the trust funds were imminent.

### Phase 3: Serial Infidelity, Gaslighting, and the Discard

While draining Cai's life savings, Bradley lived a double life. In Thailand, he pursued his neighbor, a Chinese woman named Sissy, telling her he was single and a wealthy yacht business owner. He offered her a ring and 30% of his Dubai company, which she rejected after verifying with Dubai boat builders that Bradley was penniless and not paying his invoices. Upon moving to Vietnam, Bradley quickly secured a new Vietnamese girlfriend (Ahn/Ana).

When Cai confronted Bradley about her negative bank balances (having only $6 left in her Vietnam account), he brutally gaslit her. He told her to "calm down," blamed her for his stress, and positioned himself as the victim who "actually cried last night" because she was questioning his financial delays.

> *"Once Cai firmly stated she had no more money to give, Bradley discarded her entirely. He blocked her on WhatsApp and began a smear campaign, messaging a mutual Australian friend, calling Cai a "bitch," and falsely claiming she owed him $300,000."*
