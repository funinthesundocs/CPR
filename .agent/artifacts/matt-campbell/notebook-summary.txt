## The Subject: Who is Colin James Bradley?

**Colin James Bradley**, commonly known as "**Cole**," presented himself as a marine technology visionary and innovator. Operating primarily under the company names **Marine Warrior Superyachts** (also doing business as **Finish Line Boats**), he claimed to be pioneering revolutionary "zero-fuel" and "infinite mileage" propulsion systems for luxury yachts. In reality, his entire operation was a serial cross-border fraud built on forged contracts, fictitious investors, fake bank accounts, and unpaid teams.

## The Hook: Meeting Matt Campbell & The Promises Made

**Matt Campbell**, an experienced entrepreneur with a background in renewable energy and business systemization, met **Cole** by chance on **September 6, 2025**, at a beach club in **Da Nang, Vietnam**. **Cole** was wearing a hat that boasted about infinite mileage boats, which immediately caught **Campbell's** attention.

After presenting polished but fabricated investor decks and manufacturing claims, **Cole** formally recruited **Campbell** on **September 20, 2025**, to be the Director of International Marketing. **Cole** promised **Campbell** a salary of **$40,000 per month**, equity in the company, and the authority to build out the company's global systems. To legitimize the venture, **Cole** falsely claimed to have **340 presold yacht contracts** and a massive credit line with **Zurich Bank**.

## The Nature of the Deception & What Was Taken

Work officially began on **September 24, 2025**. **Campbell** immediately dove into organizing **Cole's** chaotic business, building linked workflows, managing vendor dataflows, cleaning up proposals, and bringing in his own specialists.

However, **Cole** never paid **Campbell**. Instead, **Campbell** ended up funding the operation's survival out of his own pocket. To keep the business alive, **Campbell** paid for a company accountant and loaned **Cole** money for basic necessities, including rent, food, transport, and a moped.

Behind the scenes, **Cole's** operation was pure dysfunction. He verbally abused staff, belittled engineers, and constantly manipulated those around him. Financially, he blamed his inability to pay anyone on "softkey technical problems" with an **ADCB** bank account, falsely claiming he had **$12.5 Million USD** locked up, while simultaneously faking wire transfers through **Barclays** and **3S Money**. When pressed for proof of funds, manufacturing partners, or the **Zurich Bank** account, **Cole** could produce nothing.

## The International Scope

**Cole's** modus operandi — charm, recruit, defraud, and disappear — left a trail of destruction across multiple continents. The verified fraud spans at least four nations:

- **Australia**: **Cole** collected licensing fees from partners without delivering, leaving a trail of police reports and a default judgment of **$29,000 AUD** for unpaid accountancy fees. He also abandoned his wife, **Kelli**, in the country.
- **Thailand**: He set up workshops and hired staff, only to vanish leaving unpaid wages and rent. He is reportedly banned from re-entering the country.
- **United Arab Emirates (UAE)**: He claimed partnerships with Gulf investors using falsified documents and currently faces a default judgment of **680,000 AED** for breach of contract, which carries the threat of jail time.
- **Vietnam**: He attempted to relaunch his scam, defrauding new investors, manipulating local staff, and ultimately fleeing the country when confronted.
- **Fictitious Manufacturing**: He continuously lied about having active production lines and partnerships in **Turkey** and **China**.

## Key Evidence & Witnesses

As the lies unraveled, victims banded together to document the fraud. Key witnesses and pieces of evidence include:

- **Matt Campbell**: Served as the primary whistleblower, documenting the timeline, recording confrontations, and issuing a formal Notice of Disassociation on **October 25, 2025** to protect vendors and partners.
- **John Cooper**: The company's CFO, who collaborated with **Campbell** to expose **Cole's** fake bank transfers. **Cooper** confirmed that **Cole** had zero funds and is currently exploring pressing charges under the Modern Slavery Act, which carries a maximum sentence of **15 years in prison**.
- **Thuy Ngoc Nguyen & Ha Le Pham**: On **October 7, 2025**, **Cole** committed a sexual crime by exposing himself and demanding sex from **Nguyen**, a cleaner at an Airbnb in **Da Nang**. The incident was immediately reported to the landlord, **Pham**, and documented by **Campbell** for the local police.
- **Dave (Mancraft Yachts)**: A legitimate boat designer who rescinded **Cole's** licensing rights after discovering his fraudulent conduct and unpaid bills.
- **Documentary Evidence**: The case is supported by a mountain of forged investor agreements, fake wire receipts, unpaid invoices, and timestamped WhatsApp chat logs proving his ongoing deception.

## The Lasting Impact

**Colin James Bradley** left behind ruined reputations, unpaid livelihoods, traumatized victims, and abandoned women — including unwanted pregnancies — across the globe.

However, his actions sparked a powerful counter-movement. Refusing to be silenced, **Matt Campbell**, **John Cooper**, and dozens of other victims pooled their evidence to establish the **Court of Public Record**. By purchasing domains like **ColinJamesBradley.com** and **FinishLineBoatsScam.com**, the victims built a permanent, decentralized digital prison.

While traditional legal systems in **Vietnam**, **Australia**, and the **UAE** are actively pursuing him, the digital ledger ensures that **Cole** can no longer hide behind fake companies or flee across borders without his verifiable history following him.
