Colin James Bradley is a serial con artist who orchestrated a multi-national financial and emotional fraud syndicate under the guise of marine innovation and romantic partnership. He lured victims with the promise of revolutionary zero-fuel superyachts, lucrative business ventures, and a fabricated 150-million-euro family trust. Instead of wealth and innovation, he delivered forged contracts, unpaid wages, and absolute financial ruin to the investors, employees, and partners he exploited.

His scheme operated with mechanical precision across Australia, Thailand, the United Arab Emirates, and Vietnam. Bradley targeted financially stable individuals, embedding himself into their lives through marriage or executive recruitment, and forced them to register business entities to conceal his bankruptcies. Once in control, he systematically drained their life savings, extracted unpaid professional labor, and manufactured endless banking emergencies to justify constant cash injections. When his operations collapsed under unpaid debts, he abandoned his victims with massive legal liabilities and fled across international borders to restart the cycle.
